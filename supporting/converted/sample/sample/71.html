Go to a site that you want to pin or make a favorite.

Flash and browser add-ons
Internet Explorer can play HTML5 and many Adobe Flash Player videos, however, browser add-ons, such as Java
and Silverlight, are not supported.
Streaming audio from a website
To stream audio from a website while using other apps, do the following:

Use Internet Explorer and the other app side-by-side, or use Internet Explorer for the desktop. For more
info, see
Use apps together (side by side)
in this guide.
Music playing from the Music app continues playing when you switch apps, and when the screen turns off.
