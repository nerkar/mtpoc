Switch between open apps
If you want to keep your apps full screen, you can quickly switch between open apps.
Here’s how:
Switch back to a previous app

Touch
. Swipe in from the left edge of the screen.

Mouse
. Move the mouse pointer into the upper-left corner of the
screen, and then click.

Keyboard
. Press Alt+Tab.
Switch to a specific app

Touch
. Swipe in from the left edge, and with your finger still on the
screen, move it back toward the left edge. Tap the app you want.

Mouse
. Move the mouse pointer into the upper-left corner, and
then move it straight
down. You’ll see the apps you
recently used.
Click the app you want.

Keyboard
. Hold down the
Alt
key and press the
Tab
key repeatedly
to switch between apps. When you get to the app you want, let go.
Use apps together (side by side)
You can use two apps at the same time by snapping them side by side. This way you can see two apps on the
screen at once (such as the Mail and Calendar apps).
