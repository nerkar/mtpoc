Use apps
Surface comes with a great set of apps such as People, Skype, and Camera.
Learn more about these apps in the
Built-in apps
section of this guide.
Find and open apps
To find an app on your Surface:

Apps view
. Swipe up from the center of the Start screen (or click
on the lower-left of Start). A list of
apps appears. Type an app name or scroll to see all your apps.

Use Search
. Open the Search charm, and type an app name like Skype.
Tip

If you
don’t see
the app you want, look in the Windows Store (see
Get apps
in this guide).
