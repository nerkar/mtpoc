Lock screen app notifications
To choose which apps have notifications on the lock screen:
1.
Open the Settings charm, tap or click
Change PC settings
>
PC and devices
>
Lock screen
.
2.
Under
Lock screen apps
, choose the apps you want on your lock screen.
