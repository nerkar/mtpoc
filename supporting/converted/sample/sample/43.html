Save pages to Reading List
When you come across an article or other content that you’d like to read later, just share it to Reading List
instead of emailing yourself a link. Reading List is your personal content library. Here’s how to save a page to
Reading List:
1.
When you find a webpage that you want to read later, open the Share charm and tap or click
Reading
List
.
2.
Tap or click
Add
. A link to the content is added to Reading List.
