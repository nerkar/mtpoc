Account security
It's an excellent idea to help protect your account by adding security info to it. If you ever forget your password
or your account is hacked, we can use your security info to verify your identity and help you get back into your
account.
It’s important to
make sure you’ve added security info and check it for accuracy. For more info
, see
Microsoft
account security info: FAQ
on Windows.com.
If you think your Microsoft account has been blocked or hacked, see
Get back into your Microsoft account if it's
been blocked or hacked
on Windows.com.
