Connect devices
Connect a USB mouse, printer, and more
Surface has a full-size USB 2.0 port that you can use to connect
accessories, such as a printer, camera, music player, phone, a mouse, or
even an external hard drive.
The first time you plug in a USB accessory, Windows installs the necessary
software for you (if needed).
