Abend-AID Configuration
Part 2 contains the following chapters:
Chapter 2, “Preliminary Considerations”:
Chapter 2 describes the preliminary considerations and procedures to
complete before beginning the product configurations.
Chapter 3, “Abend-AID Viewer Configuration”:
Chapter 3 describes the Abend-AID Viewer configuration process.
Chapter 4, “Batch Abend-AID Configuration”:
Chapter 4 describes the batch Abend-AID product configuration process.
Chapter 5, “Abend-AID for CICS Configuration”:
Chapter 5 describes the Abend-AID for CICS product configuration process.
Chapter 6, “Database Support Configuration”:
Chapter 6 discusses the installation procedures for installing Abend-
AID/Abend-AID for CICS support for DB2, IDMS, and IMS.
Chapter 7, “Abend-AID Fault Analytics Configuration”:
Chapter 7 discusses how to configure the Abend-AID Fault Analytics
Collector (FAC) mainframe component.
