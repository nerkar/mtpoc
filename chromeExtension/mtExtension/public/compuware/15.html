Introduction
Intro
This manual provides the information needed to install and customize Abend-AID and
Abend-AID for CICS. It is divided into the following five main parts:
•
Part 1, “Abend-AID Receive From Network (RFN) Installation”:
Chapter 1
describes the Receive From Network (RFN) procedures for receiving the product files
and using the Compuware Installer JCL customization facility to complete the SMP/E
installation. This chapter also includes the Installation Checklists.
•
Part 2, “Abend-AID Configuration”:
Chapters 2 through 7 describe the procedures
for completing the Abend-AID, Abend-AID for CICS, Abend-AID Viewer, database,
and Abend-AID Fault Analytics configuration. Once the configuration steps have
been completed, all basic product functionality should be operational.
•
Part 3, “Abend-AID, Abend-AID for CICS, and Abend-AID Viewer
Customization”:
Chapters 8 through 23 describe additional procedures to customize
the Abend-AID, Abend-AID for CICS, and Abend-AID Viewer installation at your site.
While the customization steps are optional, you may need to do certain
customization at your site to activate desired ancillary product functionality.
•
Part 4, “Maintenance”:
Chapter 24, “Preventive Maintenance” describes the
procedures for performing product maintenance.
•
Part 5, “Appendixes”:
Appendixes A through H contain supplemental information
about controlling and customizing Abend-AID, Abend-AID for CICS, and the Abend-
AID Viewer.
A glossary and index are also included.
Intended Audience
This manual is intended for use by Abend-AID installers and systems programmers.
