Abend-AID Installation and Customization Guide
Step 8. Perform Updates to CICS
Use the appropriate RDO member for your CICS release to update the CICS system
definition (CSD) file. See “Using Resource Definition Online or the CSD Utility
DFHCSDUP” on page 5-6 for more information.
Review Chapter 5, “Abend-AID for CICS Configuration” for any additional modifications
that may be required.
Step 9. Stop and Restart the Abend-AID for CICS TDCAS
To enable support for the new release of CICS, you must stop and then restart the Abend-
AID for CICS transaction dump capture address space (TDCAS). See Appendix A,
“Controlling the Abend-AID Viewing Server, BDCAS, and TDCAS” for information about
stopping and starting Abend-AID for CICS.
